October 15, 2015

Bringing Lighting
Back to Broadway

A 10 Year Journey Tom Ruzika, IALD, IESNA |
The Ruzika Co.

  • May 2015

  • April 2015

  • March 2015

Join the DLF and The Ruzika Company's Tom Ruzika, for a 10-year journey down historic Broadway and Spring streets in downtown Los Angeles.  This presentation is a rare opportunity to look at historic buildings, building facades, original lighting techniques, and lighting design plans for restoring Broadway's prominence as a business, entertainment, and residential community.
